Recipes

You can discover the essence of the Mediterranean diet with our collection of vibrant recipes! From amazing and delicious fresh salads that'll keep you healthy to hearty nutritious pasta dishes, experience the appetizing simplicity and healthful fortune of Mediterranean cuisines, from around the world that are easy for everyone to make that even if you've never cooked before .

Soup

Asparagus Soup

This elegant asparagus soup, with its medley of spring vegetables, is ideal for the slow transition from the slumber of winter to the busy and bright summer months. But I need little excuse to make a spring soup I love to enjoy soups and stews until the temperature reaches a level that my Middle Eastern bones consider warm!




Cabbage Soup

I packed this soup with wholesome ingredients like carrots, celery, cabbage, tomatoes, and onions then seasoned everything with a dose of coriander, turmeric, cumin, and Aleppo pepper to create a harmonious blend of flavors. I finished it with a good squeeze of fresh lemon juice and a spicy kick from freshly grated ginger. It's filling, low-calorie, nutrient-rich, and feeds a crowd. I hope you love it as much as I do.




Italian lentil soup

Italians have a New Year tradition of gathering with friends and family to enjoy lenticchie. Not because they're protein-packed and nutrient-rich, though that's a nice bonus! The lentils in this Italian soup recipe symbolize abundance, as their round shape represents Roman coins expanding as they simmer. Eating lentils is believed to usher in a year of good fortune. And who wouldn't appreciate a little bit of that?!




White Bean Soup

Creamy, comforting, and loaded with classic Italian flavor, you hardly believe this hearty white bean soup is vegan and gluten-free. A humble can of cannellini beans has worked its magic yet again! All due credit goes to Ribollita, the famous Tuscan stew, for the inspiration. This variation is a vegan and gluten-free twist, which do not worry is just as flavorful and satisfying.




Salads

Herby Freekeh Salad

Freekeh doesn't get enough love! The flavorful grain is nutty, smoky, rich and filled with fiber and nutrients, and you don’t need to do much to turn it into a wow-worthy dish. I keep it simple with this easy freekeh salad recipe, allowing the superfood to do most of the talking and lifting the deeply savory notes with fresh greens, herbs, and a tart pomegranate molasses dressing.




Salmon Salad

This healthy salmon salad recipe layers on the texture and flavor with creamy Greek yogurt, peppery Dijon mustard, crunchy fresh vegetables, and fresh parsley. There's no mayo, and the salmon is baked in the oven so it could not be easier! Scoop it into lettuce cups or pile it onto bread for a sandwich.




Bean Salad

This Turkish salad recipe is satisfying and light at the same time, with a nutty, creamy tahini dressing, tender cannellini beans, biting white onions, rich hard-boiled eggs, and fresh tomatoes. It's tasty, economical, and so easy to make!




Spinach Salad

This wilted spinach salad baths baby spinach, cannellini beans, and quick-pickled onions in a warm Dijon vinaigrette. This protein and nutrition-packed warm spinach salad is finished with crunchy walnuts and tangy goat cheese—for a filling healthy dish that's main-meal worthy.




Stews

Chicken Stew

In this hearty chicken stew recipe, chicken thighs and fresh vegetables simmer in a rich tomato broth until the meat is fall-apart tender and the broth is jammy and flavorful. Italian-style seasonings like paprika, oregano, and coriander bring a delicious warming, aromatic quality. Made in just one pot, this easy recipe comes together in under an hour and freezes exceptionally well.




Greek Beef Stew

Stifado, pronounced stiff-ah-do, is a hearty Greek beef stew seasoned with warming spices, red wine, and loads of pearl onions. This stew, which is typically served as a Sunday family dinner on colder fall and winter days, is one of the many traditional Greek comfort food recipes. It's flavorful, satisfying, and mouth-wateringly delicious!




Ratatouille

This easy ratatouille recipe is my take on the classic vegetable stew from Provence, France. Tomato, eggplant, zucchini, and bell peppers simmer with aromatics until they soften, release their juices, and become jammy and delicious.




Cioppino

Cioppino (pronounced chuh-pee-no) is a famous seafood stew created by Italian-American immigrants in San Francisco. While related to many Italian and other Mediterranean fish stews this homey seafood stew recipe with halibut, shrimp, and mussels is an example of adaptation and ingenuity.




Desserts

Strawberry Pudding

Gelo di fragole, or Sicily's version of strawberry pudding, is light, refreshing, and just sweet enough! This easy, make-ahead dessert takes only 7 ingredients, zero specialty equipment, and 15 minutes or less of hands-on time. Make it the night before your next dinner party or date night in




Strawberry Shortcake

This easy strawberry shortcake recipe is made with extra virgin olive oil and whole wheat flour, which makes the biscuits not only more wholesome but extra tender and flavorful too. Citrusy sumac macerated strawberries and a dollop of tangy whipped labneh give the classic strawberry shortcake a grown-up, Mediterranean-style flavor you’ll love!




Stuffed Apricots

Luscious, sweet-and-tart poached apricots are stuffed with rich and creamy ricotta cheese and sprinkled with chopped pistachios. This Turkish-style apricot dessert recipe is deliciously fragrant and so easy to make!




Carrot Cake

I This healthy carrot cake recipe packs all the warming spice flavors of the classic, but with whole wheat flour, Greek yogurt, olive oil, dates, and honey for a lighter, more grown-up approach. To finish things off we skip the cream cheese frosting and opt for a dusting of powdered sugar instead.




Drinks

Blood Orange Rosemary Cocktail

This blood orange cocktail recipe puts yet another beautiful winter fruit center stage: the blood orange. Like mulled wine (Italian vin brulé), holiday punch or winter sangria, it’s an easy cocktail recipe to get you through those gloomy, winter months.




Holiday punch

Gathering around the Christmas punch bowl to enjoy festive holiday cocktails is a highlight of the season. This fruity, bubbly holiday punch is an explosion of flavor and so easy to make, thanks to pomegranate, cranberry, and citrus juices, aromatic orange blossom water, vodka, sparkling wine, and a touch of club soda.




Pomegranate fizz mocktail

This fruity spiced non-alcoholic pomegranate drink recipe is made with a base of honey, orange, and cardamom syrup and finished with pomegranate juice and club soda. This pomegranate mocktail is as refreshing as it is tasty!




Sgroppino italian prosecco cocktail

Icy drinks might seem like a modern invention, but today’s chilly concoction goes back hundreds of years to Venice! This updated version of a classic Sgroppino is a bright, tart Prosecco cocktail made with lemon sorbet and Vodka whipped up until frothy.
