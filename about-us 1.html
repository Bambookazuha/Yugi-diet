About us

Our objective

We plan to make a website that can be suitable for everyone and improve their diets and daily lifestyle by showing them easy and effective way of meditating, making food recipes, how to do yoga for beginners and timetable for a weak and we also have premium versions for your personal needs and guide you through your journey.




Our Scope

To implement our project into life we've made plans so firstly, we'll make four different categories which are yoga, recipes, meditation and timetable. In yoga page we'll show different yoga poses for you to do one by one to make it easier for beginners, In recipes page we'll show easy recipes that you can cook at home with basic ingredients that mostly every house will have in their refrigerator. In meditation page there will be guided videos in different minutes for beginner to advanced. In timetable there will be a timetable for one week which will guide you and help you build healthy lifestyle.




Our advantage

This project will help us to show people how they can stay healthy just by following simple habits everyday. People who follow our timetable and try doing it for a week you'll be able to feel the difference and have a healthier life.
