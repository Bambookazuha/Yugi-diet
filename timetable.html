How to follow our Timetable?

To follow our timetable effectively involves integrating yoga,meditation and recipes into your daily routine with intention and consistency. By allocating specific time slots for these practices, treating them as non-negotiable commitments, and prioritizing self-care, we create a balanced and harmonious approach to your schedule. Starting your day with yoga sets a positive tone, while incorporating moments of meditation throughout the day enhances focus and reduces stress. Embracing these practices not only enhances productivity but also nurtures your overall well-being, fostering a sense of fulfillment and balance in your life.
